# Notes for Day 5: Tuesday, 06/30/26

Why is a dictionary better than 3 separate variables?:

A dictionary can store all information of an object in one location, allowing you to easily call
specific items within the dictionary. Additionally, you can easily update the information within a dictionary or even
add new information to a dictionary.

Why is a list useful for storing multiple HGVs?:
Similar reason. A list that stores all information for all HGVs makes it easy to access needed information.

How does this relate to a digital twin?:
A digital twin is the two way relay of information. That information needs to be stored somewhere, and the amount
of information within the system can grow very rapidly. Information in a digital twin is complex, but can easily be stored
in a list. For the purposes of this project, a Digital Twin may as well be one giant list of data.
