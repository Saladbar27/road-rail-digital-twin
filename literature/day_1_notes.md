What is a digital twin?
    A Digital Twin (DT) is a digital copy of the real world or a system in the real world that uses
    sensors and signals in the real world to take in information and adjust systems accordingly. In this project,
    they're being used to decarbonize the UK specifically in transport system emissions.

    Specifically, a two way connection
    
Why use one for freight?
    Freight and the transport of goods is a complicated and interconnected system, allowing for a great
    opportunity for decarbonizing.
Why do connectivity gaps matter?
    Connectivity gaps mean less information, and with less information a DT is less able to make accurate adjustments.
